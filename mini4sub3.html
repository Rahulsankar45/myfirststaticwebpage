P. Shyama Raju is an Indian entrepreneur,
philanthropist and an educationist based in Bengaluru, Karnataka.
He is the chairman and managing director of DivyaSree,
he is also the chairman of Rukmini Educational Charitable Trust and the chancellor of REVA University, Bengaluru

 

Born: 1950 (age 73 years)

Spouse(s): Late Smt.Rukmani Shymaraju
